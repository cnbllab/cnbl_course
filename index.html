About the Workshop

This short-term course is specifically designed for UG/PG/PhD students, researchers, faculties, and technical staff interested in Computational Neuroscience, Signal Processing, AI, Transcriptomic Data Analysis, and Drug Repurposing.

About IDAPT

The Interdisciplinary Data Analytics and Predictive Technologies (IDAPT) focuses on leveraging data analytics and AI to transform healthcare, telecommunications, and power sectors under the National Mission on Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS).

About SBME

The School of Biomedical Engineering at IIT (BHU) offers unique engineering education with thrust areas in Medical Devices, Bioinstrumentation, and Computational Biology.

Course Highlights

Comp. Neuroscience

Brain function, seizures, sleep, emotion, and disease through multi-scale modeling.

MRI & EEG Analysis

Neural signal processing, brain connectivity, and cognitive state mapping.

RNA-seq Workflows

Preprocessing, differential expression, and functional annotation.

AI for Brain & Behavior

Epilepsy, emotion, and sleep decoding from multimodal data.

Drug Repurposing

Systems-level linking of molecular signatures to therapies.
